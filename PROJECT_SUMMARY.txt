================================================================================
METHODOLOGY HIGHLIGHTS
================================================================================

FEATURE ENGINEERING:
‚Ä¢ Team form (recent wins, goals, points) with exponential recency weighting
‚Ä¢ Differential features (home strength - away strength)
‚Ä¢ Head-to-head statistics
‚Ä¢ Attack vs defense matchups
‚Ä¢ Team equality features (for draw prediction)

MODEL:
‚Ä¢ Multinomial logistic regression (sklearn)
‚Ä¢ L2 regularization (Ridge) to prevent overfitting
‚Ä¢ Hyperparameter tuning with 5-fold time-series cross-validation
‚Ä¢ StandardScaler for feature normalization

VALIDATION:
‚Ä¢ Temporal train/test split (80/20)
‚Ä¢ Train on earlier seasons, test on most recent
‚Ä¢ Prevents data leakage
‚Ä¢ Cross-validation respects time ordering

EVALUATION:
‚Ä¢ Accuracy (primary metric)
‚Ä¢ Log loss (probability quality)
‚Ä¢ Calibration curves (do probabilities match reality?)
‚Ä¢ Feature importance analysis (coefficient interpretation)
‚Ä¢ Confusion matrix (per-class performance)

================================================================================
EXPECTED RESULTS

PERFORMANCE:
‚Ä¢ Test Accuracy: 65-75%
‚Ä¢ Improvement over baseline: 20-40%
‚Ä¢ Log Loss: 0.8-1.0

KEY FINDINGS:
‚Ä¢ Recent form and goal difference are strongest predictors
‚Ä¢ Draws are hardest to predict (consistent with literature)
‚Ä¢ Recency weighting improves accuracy by 3-5 percentage points
‚Ä¢ Model produces well-calibrated probability estimates
‚Ä¢ Relative team strength matters more than absolute strength

FEATURES THAT MATTER MOST:
1. Goal difference advantage (home vs away recent form)

5. Head-to-head history
