UNDERSTANDING THE OUTPUT
================================================================================

KEY METRICS EXPLAINED:

‚Ä¢ Accuracy: Percentage of correctly predicted outcomes
  - Random guessing: ~33%
  - Baseline (always predict home win): ~40-50%
  - Your model target: 65%+
  - Good performance: 70%+

‚Ä¢ Log Loss: Measures probability quality (lower is better)
  - Below 0.90: Excellent
  - 0.90-1.10: Good
  - Above 1.10: Needs improvement

‚Ä¢ Calibration: Do predicted probabilities match reality?
  - Curves close to diagonal line = well calibrated
  - Important for practical use (betting, decision-making)

INTERPRETING FEATURE IMPORTANCE:

‚Ä¢ Positive coefficient: Increases probability of that outcome
‚Ä¢ Negative coefficient: Decreases probability of that outcome
‚Ä¢ Larger magnitude: Stronger influence

Example: If "home_goal_diff" has a large positive coefficient for 'H' (Home Win),
it means teams with better recent goal difference are more likely to win at home.
